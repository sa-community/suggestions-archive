If you've ever been frustrated by not knowing when you can post on Scratch, or if you've ever accidentally tried clicking the post button again and again waiting for the cooldown to end. By graying out the post button when the cooldown is active, users will have a clear visual indicator of when they can post again, saving a lot of time. The same concept could be applied to getting muted, providing users with clear guidance on when they can start commenting on scratch again.
