Idk if #Background image in edditor is related but its rejected (not why I am here). But this is possible with CSS yeah.
document.querySelector('svg.blocklySvg').style = `
  outline: none;
  overflow: hidden;
  position: absolute;
  display: block;
  background-color: #1e1e1e55;
  background-image: url('img url');
  background-size: cover;
  background-repeat: no-repeat;
  opacity: 1;
  filter: alpha(opacity = 85);
  background-blend-mode: soft-light;
`;
