This is a revolutionary new way to add blocks in scratch.
Click (or press!) below a block to open the add blocks by name popup but filtering to actual blocks, no inputs or reporters (but these can still be in the block)
A shadow indicating you can click will show when you hover over that spot.
You can also do the same when you click on a boolean input you can also insert a boolean into it (not, =, or, etc)
When you press enter it will automatically enter it in that spot instead of following your mouse. Please upvote if you like it!
