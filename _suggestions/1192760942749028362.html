Personally, I dont think this is a good idea. Heres why:
1. Drives away traffic from non-english games
-Its no secret the largest scratch audience is english. Some genuinely amazing games I've played are from Japanese or Chinese creators, and they have an option to translate it.
2. Too much pressure on Scratch's API, like Jazza said.
Heres what I think could happen;
Have a feature in Scratch Addons to add language tags like #English and #Japanese.
Searching for those tags would allow english only speakers to find games they can play, and incentivise non-english creators to translate their work into the greater audience.
