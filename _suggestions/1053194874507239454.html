I don't know how to explain this that well, but let me give it a shot. You know if you take out a "say" block and you type something really long, the text just keeps going and going and going? Take a look at the videos. Video 2 just shows the horrible "bug" in scratch, and we have to fix this with scratch addons. And what the heck just happened in Video 3?! So here's what I propose: after the text as reached a certain width, we _word-wrap_ it so that it types in the next line instead of straight all the way. This can be really annoying for some scratchers
