I've suggested this before, but that was pre-forum days, so I'm gonna suggest it again.
This would be a huge gamepad addon update.
It would be amazing if you could select areas on the stage for the mouse to move. What I mean is, when you select an area, in a circle or a square, the pointer, controlled by a joystick, can only move in that area. Whenever you move the joystick, it auto clicks (mouse down), then moves. This would be great for the on-screen joysticks.
When you move the joystick, it will move in the selected area, as if it were a joystick, so it starts in the center of it, and goes in the direction you move it, staying inside the area you chose.
Hopefully this is understandable.

You'd start by setting an area the joystick is on the stage, it can be an area, or on a sprite (the area will be the size of the sprite, and be positioned in the middle of it). With the sprite position, it would follow the sprite.
Then when using it, it'll work by getting the position of the stick, and position the mouse in according to that spot on the stage inside the area you chose. It'll have an option to automatically click the mouse, or not when the stick is in not the neutral position. This is because sometimes you don't want the mouse to be down.
