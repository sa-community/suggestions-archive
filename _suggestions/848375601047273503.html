Brush stabilizer/brush smoother. The stabilizer (which is for bitmap) simply slows down the speed of your stroke to make it smoother, while the smoother (for vector) attempts to minimize the amount of nodes in the shape. Both of these can be configured via values that go from 0 (min) to 100 (max)
