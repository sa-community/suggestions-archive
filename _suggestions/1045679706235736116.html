Consider that you've made a beautiful image in Scratch and you want to save the colors of it to create more artwork. Or you saw someone else's creation and love the colors chosen. Sometimes, remembering is not enough. There should be an option called "create palette" that allows you to save colors, and then you should be able to name a palette and put it in your backpack.
Also, it will be compatible as other users can't look inside your backpack. Scratch Addons users won't be able to see either, but they will be able to copy your palette into their backpack, if you know what I mean.
