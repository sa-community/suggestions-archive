HOW
Click on the top variable you want to enable, and instead of letting go and moving down to the next one, keep clicking and drag down onto the other variable check boxes you want to show. (I made a video mockup showing what I mean)
WHAT
Would be a new setting to the "Data category tweaks" addon called "Drag to show reporters". (Could be a part of a separate addon because this should also work for the sensing reporters for example, hence not really data category tweaks).
WHY
If you want to show or hide more than about 3 lists or variables quickly, it takes a long time (at least for me) to enable all of them individually. Being able to show multiple with a single drag would be really nice.
