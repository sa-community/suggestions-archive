When you convert a semitransparent vector costume to bitmap it does this weird dithering you can see in the image.
If your project isn't in fullscreen this will cause an ugly flicker on your sprite as it moves.
It can be avoided by exporting as an svg, using a proper image editor(e.g. Photoshop) to rasterise it, then uploading as an HD upload of the costume for the best quality.
When your project has a lot of costumes however this gets old fast so I propose an upgrade to Convert to Bitmap to fix this.
