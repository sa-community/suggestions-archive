Have you ever wanted to follow someone without receiving activity notifications from them? Well, this idea proposes just that. It would have like a mute button next to a person's profile, and any user who is muted you will not get activity notifications on your front page from them. This would be useful for someone if they followed someone (say they were a prize-giver for a contest) but they didn't actually like their projects or want to receive activity from them. This way you can still follow them, just don't have their activity show up on your page.
